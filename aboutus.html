About Us

Located in the vibrant heart of Thamel, Kathmandu, TimberArt-Nepal has established itself as a leader in the timber and forestry industry. We are passionate about providing our customers with top-quality, sustainable timber products both locally and internationally.

Our goal is to embrace eco-friendly practices while meeting the increasing demand for high-quality timber products. Backed by a dedicated team of professionals and advanced technology, we ensure that every product we offer meets the highest standards of excellence.

At Timber Nepal, we strive to create a harmonious balance between progress and the environment. Join us on our journey toward a greener, more sustainable future.
